# Privacy Policy for PluckGPT

**Effective Date:** April 18, 2026

PluckGPT (“the App”) is developed by Lonely Star Software, Inc. (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or account information.

However, the App may access or process the following:

### a. Camera and Photos

The App uses your device’s camera to scan images. Images are processed on your device and are not collected or stored by us.

### b. Location Data

The App may optionally access your device’s location if you grant permission. Location access is not required to use the App, and we do not store or transmit your location data.

### c. Contacts

The App may allow you to create contacts, but it does not access, read, or upload your existing contacts.

### d. In-App Purchase Information

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat may process purchase-related data (such as transaction receipts and subscription status) to provide this functionality. This data is handled in accordance with RevenueCat’s privacy practices.

## 2. Data Storage

All user data created within the App is stored locally on your device and may be synced to your personal iCloud account. This data is not accessible to us and is controlled entirely by you through your Apple account.

We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

## 3. Tracking and Advertising

We do not use advertising services and do not track users across apps or websites.

We do not collect device identifiers for tracking purposes.
